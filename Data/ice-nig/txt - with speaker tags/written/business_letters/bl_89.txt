WORKSHOP ON ENHANCING CRITICAL AND CREATIVE THINKING 
    THROUGH READING AND WRITING
The Centre for Distance Learning is organizing a 3-day intensive 
    workshop for Programme instructors on enhancing critical and 
    creative thinking through reading and writing.
The overall objective of the workshop is to enhance the quality 
    of all programmes of the Centre for Distance Learning The workshop 
    which is in collaboration with the Reading Association of Nigeria, 
    lle-lfe chapter, will cover various topics with two facilitators 
    from USA ( 
     National Writing project, Boston 
    and  
     Saint Cloud State University, Minneapolis) and one from Nigeria 
    (X. X.  
    Dept Of English, O.A.U, 
    lle-lfe). We will use the workshop to also expose the participants 
    to the Video conferencing technology, as one of the techniques in 
    Open and Distance Electronics Learning mode.
The workshop which will commence on Monday, 
    13th August, 2007 at 10.00am will 
    end on Wednesday 15th August, 2007 
    
The venue is the Conference room of the Centre for Distance 
    Learning. Kindly attend the workshop.
Thank you.

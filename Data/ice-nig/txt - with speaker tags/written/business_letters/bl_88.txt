SER 001: USE OF ENGLISH
This is to bring to your notice that SER 001: Use of 
    English is a 4-unit course, which is meant to be 
    registered in the Harmattan Semester. However, it is a sessional 
    course because the examination is taken at the end of the Harmattan 
    Semester.
During the registration of course in the Harmattan Semester 
    2006/2007, some head of departments complained to the Department of 
    English that their Part I students would not be able to register 
    for SER 001 because it would make the number of units of the 
    courses they register to overshoot the required maximum of 24 
    units. In order to accommodate such students, the Department 
    decided to split the 4 units for SER 001, thereby allowing such 
    students to register for 2 units in the Harmattan Semester and 2 
    units in the Rain Semester However, for those 
    who were able to accommodate the 4 units, they would not need to 
    register again in the Rain Semester.
It was however brought to our notice this semester that the 
    course still reflects 4 units for the Rain semester. What this 
    translates into is that those who registered for 2 units in the 
    Harmattan and now register for the course in the Rain semester will 
    now have a total of 6 units. This should not be so. The Department 
    also learnt because of the inability of some students to resolve 
    the issue of the number of units, they could not register for the 
    course in the Rain Semester.
In view of the fact that some students who registered for the 
    course in the Harmattan Semester could not register for it in the 
    Rain Semester, the Department is requesting the Computer Centre to 
    help to resolve this issue by automatically registering all those 
    who registered 2 units in the Harmattan Semester, so that they can 
    complete the required number of units for the course.
Thank you.

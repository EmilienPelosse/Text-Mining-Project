<p> <ICE-JA:W2F-015#109:2> Before that outing Ossie had never seen the sea.
<ICE-JA:W2F-015#110:2> He had heard grown-ups talk about it, and they had described it as a

<ICE-JA:W2F-015#112:2> Perhaps the best description he got was from an old man who said it

<p> <ICE-JA:W2F-015#113:2> One year, a few weeks before Easter, a man named Mass Aston went

<p> <ICE-JA:W2F-015#117:2> The day before the outing he got up early and completed all his

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#68:2> <h> <bold> THE RESERVED BOOK COLLECTION ( RBC) </bold> </h> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#69:2> <h> <bold> What is RBC? </bold> </h> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#70:2> RBC is a collection of reserved books recommended by lecturers

from undergraduate reading lists. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#71:2> <h> <bold> What is the purpose of RBC? </bold> </h> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#72:2> RBC aims to provide necessary reading material to large numbers

of students in classes of various sizes.
<ICE-JA:W2D-010#73:2> The loan period is shorter than normal to allow access by the

greatest number of students. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#74:2> <h> <bold> What is the loan period? </bold> </h> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#75:2> Usually overnight, reading room, and three hour loan. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#76:2> <h> <bold> Are there enough books for each student in a course?

</bold> </h> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#77:2> Multiple copies are purchased for courses with large numbers of

students, and for items which serve more than one course.
<ICE-JA:W2D-010#78:2> However, such copies are often not enough to satisfy the demand.
<ICE-JA:W2D-010#79:2> Therefore the more quickly items are returned, the more quickly they

become available to others. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#80:2> <h> <bold> Who can borrow from the RBC </bold> </h> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#81:2> All registered students of the University who have also been

registered in the library. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#82:2> <h> <bold> How does the issuing system work? </bold> </h> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#83:2> RBC operates on a <it> first come, first served </it> basis so a

numbering system is used for the issuing of items.
<ICE-JA:W2D-010#84:2> Failure to obtain an item might mean that all copies have been issued

out already.
<ICE-JA:W2D-010#85:2> It might also mean that the item was never recommended by your

lecturer, and therefore is not in RBC. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#86:2> <h> <bold> How can you help? </bold> </h> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#87:2> Make sure the item you need is in <bold> RBC </bold> by checking

the catalogue. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#88:2> Fill out the Reading Room/Overnight Loan forms correctly. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#89:2> Have your UWI ID card as well as your library registration card

at all times. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#90:2> Return items promptly so that as many students as possible can

have access to these items. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#91:2> <h> <bold> Are there penalties for the late return of books?

</bold> </h> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-010#92:2> <bold> Yes. </bold>
<ICE-JA:W2D-010#93:2> Fines are charged for overdue books <ul> by the hour </ul> for 3 hour

loan Reading Room books and <ul> by the day </ul> for Overnight books.
<ICE-JA:W2D-010#94:2> Please check at the Circulation Desk for current charges. </p> </I>

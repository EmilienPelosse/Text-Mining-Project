Fashola Calls for Media Partnership with Three Estates of 
    the Realm
Written by Adeleke Afolayan
Lagos State Governor Babatunde Fashola has asked for a dynamic 
    partnership between between the 
    media and the other three estates of the realm in order to avoid 
    animosity in governance.
Speaking at the biennial convention of the National Guild of 
    Editors at the Ikeja Airport Hotel, Governor Fashola said in the 
    absence of such partnership, the government would not function 
    effectively.
Fashola, who was presented with two plagues ew: 
    wrong choice of words - means "honour; award" 
    etc. by Vanguard Newspaper Publisher Sam Pemu for 
    his friendship with the Guild and achievements so far in the State, 
    said that with the media as fourth estate, working with the 
    goverment would enable a flow 
    of right information to the public and consequently the sustainable 
    development of the State.
According to him, the secret behind countries like South Africa 
    maintaining an excellent international image despite negative 
    happenings was a function of their media which plays down such 
    incidences.

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#4:1> 2 ozs. cracked black peppercorns <space> 4 ozs. white cane vinegar

</p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#5:1> 2 ozs. escallion <space> 8 ozs. brown beef stock </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#6:1> 3 ozs. fever-grass <space> A dash salt </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#7:1> 2 ozs. garlic <space> 5 ozs. cooking oil </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#8:1> <bold> Heat heavy bottom fry pan with cooking oil.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#9:1> Saut&eacute; escallion, peppercorns, fever-grass, garlic and ginger

for 15 seconds until sweated.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#10:1> Add brown sugar and cook until sugar is partially dissolved.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#11:1> Add vinegar, beef stock and Dragon Stout and bring to a boil.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#12:1> Remove from heat and put over the raw top round.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#13:1> Place the mixture in a 350 degree F oven and braise for 40 minutes.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#14:1> Remove from oven, cool down and place mixture in the refrigerator and

let marinate for 24 hours.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#15:1> After marinating, drain the beef and toss with blanched broccoli and

tomato slivers and serve on a salad plate with a garnish of lentil sprouts

and watercress. </bold> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#68:1> <h> <bold> BEEF WITH RED STRIPE BEER AND CALLALOO </bold> </h>

</p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#69:1> 10 slices bacon ( chopped) <space> 2 tbsps. brown sugar </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#70:1> 2 lbs. beef cross-rib ( cut in <w> 1" </w> pieces) <space> 1/2

tsp. red pepper ( dry, crushed) </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#71:1> 6 ozs. onions ( minced) <space> 2 ozs. cider vinegar </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#72:1> 4 cloves garlic ( minced) <space> 8 ozs. callaloo ( clean, cut in

strips) </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#73:1> 2 ozs. flour <space> Salt and black pepper </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#74:1> 1 qt. Red Stripe Beer </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#75:1> <bold> Fry bacon until lightly browned, remove pieces, reserve.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#76:1> Remove all but 2 ozs. of the fat reserve the remainder.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#77:1> Add beef and brown on all sides, remove, keep warm.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#78:1> Add onions and garlic, saut&eacute;, add flour and enough of the

bacon fat to form a gravy, cook over medium heat 2-3 minutes stirring

constantly.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#79:1> Add Red Stripe Beer, sugar, vinegar and pepper, bring to a simmer,

whisking until smooth.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#80:1> Add meat and bacon pieces.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#81:1> In a separate pan saut&eacute; the callaloo until wilted, add to the

beef, return to a simmer.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#82:1> Cover the pan and braise in a 350 degree F oven until meat is tender

( aprox. 1-1/2 hours) stir occasionally.
<ICE-JA:W2D-015#83:1> When meat is done, degrease, and adjust seasonings. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-015#84:1> N.B.: You can substitute pork or lamb for the beef. </bold> </p>

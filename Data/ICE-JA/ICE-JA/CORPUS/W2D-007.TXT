<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-007#4:1> <h> <bold> DEFINITION </bold> </h> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-007#5:1> The Writing Portfolio is a collection of specified pieces of <w>

students' </w> written work related to the course objectives.
<ICE-JA:W2D-007#6:1> The pieces are prepared over time so that students may benefit from

the drafting, rewriting, reflecting, refining and perfecting stages which are

components of the normal writing process. </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-007#7:1> Each Portfolio will contain: </p>

<p> &bullet; answers to all assigned short answer questions and practice

exercises </p>

<p> &bullet; two or more drafts of assigned essays and reports </p>

<p> &bullet; weekly one-page reflections in which students explore and

attempt to evaluate their learning/writing experiences with particular units

or assignments </p>

<p> &bullet; self-assigned tasks indicating each student's efforts at self

improvement </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-007#8:1> <h> <bold> HOW TO PREPARE THE PORTFOLIO </bold> </h> </p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-007#9:1> &bullet; Obtain a flat folder or file jacket ( 24x30cm) and ruled,

<ICE-JA:W2D-007#13:1> Leave ample space at the end of each task for the tutor's comments.

</p>

<p> <ICE-JA:W2D-007#14:1> &bullet; All entries should be typed or legibly handwritten and

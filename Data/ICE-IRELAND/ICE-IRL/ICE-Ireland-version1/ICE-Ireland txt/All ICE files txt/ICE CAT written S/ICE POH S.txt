drink, said to be both potent and delicious. <#> It was made from honey, clear sparkling water and aromatic plants. <#> Bragget, made by fermenting ale and honey together, was also much enjoyed for it were lauded by the poets. <#> St Brigid, that redoubtable woman who knew how to influence prelates and princes, is said to have given the King of Leinster a cup of mead to drink when he came to visit

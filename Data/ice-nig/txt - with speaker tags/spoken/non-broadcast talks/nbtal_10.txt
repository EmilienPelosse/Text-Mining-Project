tribute to my grandfather Papa Nnukwu when I heard of your exit of this sinful world it pained me so much this is because even though you were old sick and bedridden you were still able to recognise everyone that comes around you and could still engage such in a meaningful conversation it was indeed a delight talking with you well we cannot question God who is all loyal Papa we will indeed miss you you were a man of the people a peace lover a mentor to many a community leader an opinion moulder you lived a very sacrificial life as you would spend your last kobo to give relief to the trouble you were fondly called by those who love you and those who have benefitted from your benevolence Emeriaku you were hard-working and a master of your work your mastery of mason work made you number one choice of every intending builder your devotion to the service of God is unequalled the church where you worshipped is many miles away but you never missed service for once no matter the weather situation or your health condition except when your feet could no longer carry you even then you continued to say your prayers without ceasing even in your sickbed mhm Papa how can we handle the vacuum your exit has created in our hearts we have resolved to take consolation in the exemplary life you lived the legacy l- you left for us will be our watchword your values we will uphold your advices we will follow your dreams we will live out your achievements we will consolidate adieu Papa you have gone but never forgotten your memories will ever remain fresh in our mind until we see again on that resurrection morning may the merciful Lord grant your gentle soul eternal rest good night Papa

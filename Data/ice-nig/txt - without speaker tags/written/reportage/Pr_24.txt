Goodluck Jonathan Buries Cousin
Written by Adeleke Afolayan
Vice President Goodluck Jonathan months 
    after laying his father to rest was back in 
    his hometown Otuoke in Ogbia Local Government of Bayelsa State to 
    bury his paternal cousin Mrs. Harriet Agbalayam Digha.
Spaeking at the burial, he 
    described her as an amiable and friendly person whom he had a very 
    close relationship with since his father and her mother were the 
    only children of their parents.
Officiating Pastor at the interment, Pastor Joe Agbaje of the 
    Christ Embassy Church in Yenagoa said Mrs. Digha was a 
    devout Christian who cared for those around her, urging others to 
    make an example of her.
The ceremony was attended by the Vice President's wife Dame 
    Patience Jonathan, Deputy Bayelsa State Governor Hon. 
    Peremebowei Ebebi, Speaker of the State House of Assembly among 
    other dignitaries.
Mrs. Digha was a successful businesswoman and passed on a 
    fortnight ago at the age of 51.

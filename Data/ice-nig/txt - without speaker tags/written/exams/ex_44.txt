My main purpose of writing this letter is to complain about the 
    poor states of our roads in this local government and to inform you 
    of the conseq-uences of it.
Sir, it will interest you to know that 75 per cent of our roads 
    in this local government are in a bad condition. And as a result of 
    that economic activities are not moving on well in our area 
    on like other 
    nabouring local governments 
    where businesses are moving fine. Our roads are very bad, 
    Aaccidents rate is on the increase on a daily 
    bases,  a period of six month we recorded 
    fourty six cases of accidents
    loseing 
    about twentynine lives and 
    it is not good for us as a people in this local government. Our 
    businesses are seriously affected by the roads. Traders and market 
    women are complaining that their businesses are not moving on fine. 
    Road transport workers are taking advantage of the situation to 
    increase the price of transporting goods to areas affected and it 
    is not good for our areas 
    economy. Sir I am plead on behalf of our 
    market women and every one affected for a change and a 
    reabilitation of our 
    roads in the local government.
Sir I understand you have done 
    alot for our community since your 
    administration and I believe you can still do more for our people. 
    And I pray that God protect and preserve you for us in 
    Jesuse Name
